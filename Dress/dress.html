The Intersection of Biology and Byte.

Fashion has traditionally been a game of approximation. S, M, L—these are blunt tools for a complex world. At Corner Street, we believe the interface between the human body and the world should be intelligent. By utilizing LiDAR scanning and generative adversarial networks (GANs), we create garments that respect the unique topography of your existence.

Zero-Waste via Algorithm.

Traditional fashion is wasteful. Fabric is cut, and scraps are discarded. Our AI nesting algorithms calculate the most efficient pattern layout possible, reducing fabric waste by 40%. We use lab-grown cotton and recycled ocean plastics to spin our "Titanium-Silk" blends, ensuring that your style doesn't cost the Earth.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Sizing work?

You use the Corner Street App to perform a 10-second LiDAR scan of your body. Our neural engine then generates a 3D mesh and tailors the pattern to your exact dimensions within 0.5mm accuracy.

What is the return policy on AI-custom items?

Because these items are made specifically for you, we offer a "Fit Guarantee." If it doesn't fit, we will alter it for free. Full refunds are available only for manufacturing defects.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 40 countries. Shipping times vary based on the complexity of the garment generation.
